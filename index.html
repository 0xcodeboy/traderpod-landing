03 — How it works

Three steps. Two minutes.

01

Connect MetaTrader

Plug in your MT4 or MT5 account using your investor (read-only) password. Pre-loaded server lists for Exness, JustMarkets, XM, FXTM, OctaFX, HFM. We can never trade your account — we only watch it.

02

Set your rules

Pick from 15 pre-built behavioral rules across risk management, psychology, and discipline. Set your max risk, your daily loss limit, your max trades per day. Your discipline framework, locked in.

03

Get intervened

Every 15 seconds, the discipline engine scores your account. The moment a rule is in danger, push notifications fire — calm at first, then escalating. You'll know before you click. That's the whole point.
